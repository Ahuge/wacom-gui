Stylus

  • Alter pressure sensitity
  • Switch between Absolute and Relative mode
  • Swap modes of side buttons on stylus
  • Switch between 'hover' or 'touch pad' to click (Pen Touch)

Eraser

  • Alter pressure sensitity
  • Switch between Absolute and Relative mode

Pad

  • Assign key presses to Pad buttons
    • Click on a button, press keys and click on same button
  • Disable a button
    • Click on a button, press 'Esc' key then '0' (zero) key and click on same button

Touch

  • Enable/Disable Finger Touch capability

Other Settings

  • Map tablet to left, right or both monitors (if more that one)
  • Switch between right and left handed operation
  • Restore all settings to the system defaults

General Notes

  • The keys 'Win' plus 'z' (pressed together) toggles the tablet mapping between left, right and both monitors. Can be changed via 'Keyboard Shortcuts'
